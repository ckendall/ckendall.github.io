My new site

2-5-15

Alright all, Phase-0 in DBC is coming to an end and I am getting a little scared to start this new phase of my life, but excited at the same time. For my last assignment I wanted to redo my whole website. I wasn't happy with it and it just so happened that one of the assignments was to redo it as well. I really enjoyed the entire process - from the beginning making a website and then redoing it because it looked, to me, terrible. Then redoing the CSS and the HTML. I know that I could go a little further with the HTML, but that time will come. What was hard was figuring out how everything moved and how to change the colors to what looked good and where to put everything. I guess everything was a little challenging. I learned everything I know from developing from these 9-weeks. I know it's not too much, but it is way more than what I knew from the beginning. Well, next time I talk to you all I will be in the middle of the program and learning a ton. Have a good couple weeks until then! Cody
